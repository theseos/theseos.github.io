It always seems impossible until it's done.
The Seo Family landed at the Toronto Pearson International Airport in 2017, and they found that Canada is one of the best places to raise a family. Essentially, living in Canada provides the perfect backdrop to teach children how to live harmoniously with people from different backgrounds. They are fortunate enough to be new Canadian citizens and are grateful for every experience they have ever had as new immigrants. They would like to say the biggest thank you to parents, family, friends, neighbours, teachers, and everyone around them.
